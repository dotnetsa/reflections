1. What happens when you initialize a repository? Why do you need to do it?
It creates a .git folder (hidden), to save the versions of each commit.

2. How is the staging area different from the working directory and the repository? What value do you think it offers?
The staging area has the files that will be committed to the working directory. 
All files don't need to be committed, but only the ones added to the staging area.
